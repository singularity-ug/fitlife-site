Fitlife Solutions Mission

Our dedicated mission is your healthy life

Fit Life Solutions is on a double mission:
Firstly; to contribute to the development of a proactive and healthy lifestyle in the Ugandan community through the provision of extra ordinary recreational and educational services.
Secondly; to minimize / prevent drowning in Uganda through provision of lifesaving services and water safety awareness and outreach.

Who we are

Get to know about our company

Fit Life Solutions is a Ugandan based Company which was established to contribute to a healthy lifestyle of the Ugandan community through the provision of unique recreational, sports and fitness services, and to minimize drowning and water-related injuries through provision of swimming and lifesaving services, as well as water safety awareness and outreach.
Living a healthy lifestyle can help prevent chronic diseases and long-term illnesses.
